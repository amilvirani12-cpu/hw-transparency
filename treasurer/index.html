What these flags are — and are not. Every row below is an automated screening flag: a consistency check that did not tie out exactly when this tool re-computed the district's own published numbers (roll-forwards, printed totals, cross-page reconciliations, collateral floors). They are not audit findings, conclusions, or allegations — most flags have routine explanations (timing, rounding, presentation changes), and items are pending review. Each flag shows the exact expected vs. printed figures so anyone can verify it against the source report.
